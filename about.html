Founder

Mr. Yevhen Kondratchuk holds a specialist degree in International Relations and Languages from the International Institute of Linguistics and Law, Kyiv, Ukraine. Apart from his carrier in leading national Ukrainian legal companies Mr. Kondratchuk was engaged in pro-bono supporting activities for a new generation of Ukrainian IT startups by providing a formation support, an early stage business advice, as well as drafting of legal templates' and solving operational on-going legal metters. A close cooperation with IT talents, a high growth capacity of IT industry, its innovative nature and sustainable development have inspired Mr. Kondratchuk to accomplish a course of Project Management in IT thus shifted his professional interests into the innovative IT industry.
